# Doshok

Doshok is a Bangladesh-based women's fashion ecommerce store. The platform enables browsing women's clothing (three-piece sets, salwar kameez, dresses, tops, occasion wear), managing a shopping cart, checking out with COD, tracking orders, and contacting support. Deliveries are nationwide across Bangladesh.
